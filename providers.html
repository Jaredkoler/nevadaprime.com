Required Documentation

  • Physician prescription / order for pneumatic compression device
  • ICD-10 diagnosis code(s) for lymphedema or qualifying condition
  • Face-to-face evaluation notes documenting medical necessity
  • History of conservative treatment (e.g., manual lymph drainage, compression garments, elevation)
  • Patient measurements for garment sizing (we can assist with this)
  • Patient insurance information and demographics
